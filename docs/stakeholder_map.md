# Stakeholder Map

| Stakeholder | Interest | Influence | What They Need | Engagement |
|---|---|---:|---|---|
| Maintenance planners | Prioritized intervention queue and confidence in recommendations | High | Clear risk tiers, action owner, and ability to override | Weekly build reviews and UAT |
| Asset engineers | Technical validity and asset-specific thresholds | High | Sensor features, failure modes, and threshold rationale | Design workshops and model review |
| Operations control | Service reliability and disruption reduction | High | Impact on incidents, delays, and capacity | Pilot steering group |
| Data engineering | Reliable data pipelines and source ownership | Medium | Source schema, refresh SLA, data quality rules | Data readiness workstream |
| Data science / ML | Model performance and monitoring | Medium | Feature pipeline, validation metrics, drift signals | Model governance forum |
| Safety and compliance | Safe use of decision-support outputs | High | Limitations, controls, human approval, audit trail | Formal approval gate |
| Senior sponsor | Business case and delivery confidence | High | Benefits, risks, timeline, go/no-go recommendation | Milestone updates |
| Frontline technicians | Practical maintenance work and trust in outputs | Medium | Work order context and feedback route | Training and feedback sessions |

## Communication Rhythm

- Steering update: every two weeks.
- Technical working group: weekly.
- Planner UAT: at each dashboard release candidate.
- Post-pilot review: after four weeks of shadow-mode operation.
